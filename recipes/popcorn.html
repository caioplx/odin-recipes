Popcorn

Description

Popcorn is a classic snack. Salty and crunchy, it's the perfect treat for a movie night. Easy to make, and even easier when you have a microwave.

Ingredients

  • Bag of microwave popcorn.

Steps

  1. Take off plastic wrapping on microwave popcorn bag.
  2. Open microwave.
  3. Put bag into microwave and set time for 2 minutes.
  4. Listen to popcorn popping.
  5. Take the bag out and open according to instructions on bag.
