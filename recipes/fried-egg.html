Fried Egg

Description

Eggs are tasty. Oil is tasty. Combine the two and you get an even tastier egg.

Ingredients

  • One raw egg
  • Oil fit for human consumption

Steps

  1. Crack open egg in SEPARATE container
  2. Add oil to pan and turn on heat to medium-high.
  3. Let it preheat for a minute.
  4. Add egg.
  5. Let egg cook to desired doneness. Flip over after bottom has solidified if sunny side eggs are not desired.
  6. Turn heat off.
  7. Serve egg.
